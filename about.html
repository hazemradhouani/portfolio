About

HazemRadhouani

Architect · Heritage & Rehabilitation · International Practice

Architect graduated with Highest Honours (ENAU, 2026), drawn to the architecture of the existing: the inhabited, the deteriorated, the overlooked. My practice spans Paris, Casablanca, and Tokyo, cities where I have read how buildings hold memory, resist erasure, and remain rooted in place. I am determined to make heritage rehabilitation a speciality, and the breadth of my work reflects that commitment at every scale, from regional rail infrastructure in Morocco to residential rehabilitation in Paris and comparative heritage research in Japan.
