About

I set ideas into motion.

I am an architect and motion designer based between Paris, Casablanca, and Tunis. My motion work grows directly from architectural practice. A background in reading space, structure, and material informs how I think about timing, camera, and the choreography of ideas on screen.

I work in Adobe After Effects and Adobe Illustrator, and trained through the School of Motion curriculum, a rigorous education that shaped my understanding of easing, anticipation, and the principles that make movement feel right rather than simply correct.

My output ranges from architectural visualisation, where precision and spatial coherence are paramount, to motion graphic reels exploring pattern, texture, and abstraction as a graphic language of their own.
